Solution to Rabies

The solution for the disease rabies is a mobile clinic that is connected to a system of phone booths with first aid kit and a dog capturing device. The purpose of this is to boost the availability of tests and treatment for rabies.

Directions

  1. If a person spots a dog or is bitten by a dog, they would then use the phone booth to call for help.
  2. Then they would proceed with the dog capturing device or use the first aid kit to disinfect the wound.
  3. The mobile clinic would then go to their location and examine the person that has been bitten as well as examine the dog.
  4. After the dog will be brought to the shelter where it can be vaccinated.
